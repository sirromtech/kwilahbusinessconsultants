About Us

Empowering Businesses Across Papua New Guinea

Kwilah Limited is a proudly Papua New Guinean-owned enterprise based in East New Britain and Port Moresby, delivering high-impact services that empower communities and drive sustainable development. Our model blends traditional sectors—construction, agriculture, and fisheries—with modern consultancy to support SMEs and local industries.

We offer a full range of SME support services including IPA & IRC registration, logo design, branding, business profiles, letterheads, bank account setup, tax compliance, advisory, and proposal development. Our mission is to create employment and training opportunities for youth while delivering affordable, high-quality services.

Core Values: Integrity, Empowerment, Innovation, Client-Centered Service, and Transparency.
