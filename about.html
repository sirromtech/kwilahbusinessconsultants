Company Overview

Locally Rooted. Nationally Driven.

  • Business Name: Kwilah Limited
  • Structure: Sole Proprietorship
  • Location: East New Britain Province and National Capital District
  • Established: 2025
  • Owner & Director: Mr. Don David

Kwilah Limited is a locally owned business with diverse services tailored to meet the evolving needs of the people in East New Britain and Papua New Guinea. Our business model blends traditional sectors such as construction and agriculture with modern consultancy solutions, creating a multi-service platform that supports development and self-reliance.

Vision, Mission & Values

Vision

To become a well-established and reputable company in East New Britain and expand operations throughout Papua New Guinea.

Mission

To be an agent of change by creating training and employment opportunities for youths, while delivering high-quality and affordable services to all clients.

Core Values

  • Integrity and Professionalism
  • Empowerment through Employment and Training
  • Innovation and Adaptability
  • Client-Centered Service
  • Accountability and Transparency

Goals & Objectives

Goals

  • Establish a reputable brand across the province and the country
  • Expand service offerings and client base within five years
  • Create employment and income-generating opportunities for local youth

Objectives

  • Successfully execute construction and civil works projects
  • Deliver reliable business consultancy services for SMEs
  • Support local seafood and cocoa industries through sustainable procurement and supply
  • Increase production capacity in poultry and cocoa farming
